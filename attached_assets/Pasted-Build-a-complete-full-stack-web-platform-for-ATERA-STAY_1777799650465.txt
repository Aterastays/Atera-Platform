Build a complete full-stack web platform for ATERA STAYS — a UK property 
company operating two services: Atera Stays (Rent-to-Serviced 
Accommodation / company let model) and Atera Management (full property 
management for landlords). The legal entity is ATERA INDUSTRIES LTD 
(England & Wales) but the trading brand shown throughout is ATERA Stays.
The platform has two parts: a public-facing storytelling landing page 
and a password-protected internal operations hub.

NO deal sourcing functionality anywhere in the platform.

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
The landing page = Apple product storytelling.
One message per section. Cinematic whitespace. Text animates in 
on scroll. Story guides the visitor. No clutter.

The hub = iOS precision.
Like the iPhone Settings app. One primary action per screen.
Information in digestible groups. Status shown through colour.
A new user understands any page within 10 seconds.
Works identically on a 390px iPhone and a 1440px desktop — 
the layout adapts but no features are hidden or broken on mobile.

RESPONSIVE
Mobile:   < 768px   single column
Tablet:   768–1024  two column where appropriate
Desktop:  > 1024px  full layout
